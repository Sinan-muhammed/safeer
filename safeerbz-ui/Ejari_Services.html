Frequently Asked Questions

What is Ejari and why do I need it?

Ejari is the official lease registration system in Dubai. It's required for legal lease contracts and provides formal recognition of tenancy agreements. It's essential for both residential and commercial property rentals.

How long does Ejari registration take?

Ejari registration is typically processed quickly, usually within a few working days. We handle all documentation to ensure fast and efficient processing through the Dubai Lands Department.

What documents are needed for Ejari registration?

Required documents typically include passport copies, visa copies, property documentation, and lease agreement details. Our team will guide you on specific requirements based on your transaction type.

Can I cancel an Ejari contract?

Yes, Ejari contracts can be canceled. We provide professional assistance with the cancellation process through the proper channels and documentation requirements.

Do you provide property valuation services?

Yes, we offer professional valuation services for real estate units including apartments, offices, and shops. Our valuations are recognized by the Dubai Lands Department.
