Mainland License Options

Types of Mainland Business Licenses

Compare common mainland license structures to see which route best supports trading, service delivery, or lean digital operations.

Commercial License

Starting

AED 12,900

  • Suitable for trading, import, export, and general commercial activity.
  • Supports businesses that sell products across local and regional markets.
  • Works well for retail, wholesale, and distribution-led models.
  • Can support branch expansion as operations grow.
  • Provides a strong base for businesses with broader market ambitions.
Start Registration

Professional License

Starting

AED 12,000

  • Designed for consultants, agencies, specialists, and service-led firms.
  • Works well for businesses built around expertise rather than product trading.
  • Commonly used by advisory, creative, education, and technical teams.
  • Supports flexible service delivery across the UAE.
  • Ideal for boutique operators that want a scalable professional base.
Start Registration

Instant E-Trader License

Starting

AED 1,070

  • Best suited to home-based and digital-first business models.
  • Offers a simpler route for solo founders and lean online operations.
  • Requires a lighter initial setup compared with larger company structures.
  • Works well for early-stage businesses testing demand and operations.
  • Helps founders launch with minimal administrative complexity.
